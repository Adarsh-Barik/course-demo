# Logistics


**Evaluation**: The course evaluation policy is:

- Minor:  20%
- Major:  30%
- Lab exams:  13% (2 exams)
- Lab completion: 10%
- In-class Quizzes: 15% (3 quizzes)
- Python Assessment/4th Week Quiz:  5%   (Maximum of the scores obtained in this quiz and the test conducted in July 2026 will be considered.)
- Attendance:  2% (lectures) + 5% (labs)
